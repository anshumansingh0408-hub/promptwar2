How to Register as a Voter 📝

Step-by-step guide to getting your voter ID and exercising your democratic right in Indian elections.

🎯

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Indian citizen by birth or naturalization
✓ 18 years or above as on January 1 of the qualifying year
✓ Ordinary resident of the constituency
✓ Not declared of unsound mind by a competent court
✓ Not disqualified under any law relating to corrupt practices
📄

Documents Required

📅 Age Proof (any one)

  • • Birth Certificate
  • • 10th Class Marksheet
  • • Passport
  • • PAN Card

🏠 Address Proof (any one)

  • • Aadhaar Card
  • • Passport
  • • Utility Bill (electricity, water, gas)

📷 Passport Photo

Recent passport-size photograph

🆔 Aadhaar Number

Optional but recommended for linking

🌐

Online Registration Steps

1

Visit voterportal.eci.gov.in

Go to New Voter Registration → Fill Form 6

2

Enter Personal Details

Name, date of birth, address, mobile number

3

Upload Documents & Photo

Age proof, address proof, and passport-size photo

4

Submit & Note Reference ID

Save your reference ID to track application status

5

BLO Verifies Details

Booth Level Officer visits your address for verification

6

EPIC Card Delivered

Card delivered to your address or collect from ERO office. You can also download e-EPIC.

🏢

Offline Registration

1. Collect Form 6 from the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) or BLO
2. Submit the filled form along with required documents
3. BLO visits your address for physical verification
4. Processing takes 30-45 days after verification
📋

What is SIR (Summary Revision)?

• Annual exercise to update and clean up electoral rolls

• January 1st qualifying date — if you turn 18 by Jan 1, you become eligible

• New voters register, existing voters update their details (address, photo, etc.)

• Conducted by ECI before major elections to ensure accurate voter lists

👤

What is a BLO?

Booth Level Officer — a government official assigned to each polling booth area
Maintains voter rolls — adds new voters, updates records, removes deceased
Door-to-door verification — visits homes to verify voter registration applications
Contact via ERO or 1950 — reach your BLO through the helpline or local ERO office
